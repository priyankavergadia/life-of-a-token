# Vibe Coding — For Everyone (no coding needed)

A plain-language overview of the **Vibe Code Your Business Idea**
assignment: plan a real app, prompt an AI to build it, and ship a live product.

👉 **Open it:** https://priyankavergadia.github.io/life-of-a-token/#/vibe-coding

---

## The two parts
1. **Prompt Development (Plan & Design)** — before any building, you architect the
   idea: what problem, who it's for, what to build, and a clear "Master Prompt" to
   hand the AI. Work the 5 phases: Discovery → Planning → Prompt Architecture →
   Polish Criteria → Handoff Plan.
2. **Project Creation (Build & Ship)** — turn the plan into a **live, deployed app**
   with real sign-in, real data, a clean look, and a confident live demo.

## What "excellent" looks like
A crisp business problem, a flawless small set of features (no crashes), real
auth + data, polished UX, staged AI prompting, a 1-page user guide, and a live
demo on real data.

## How it's graded (100 pts)
- **Plan 30** · **Build 35** · **Present 30** · **Peer 5**

## Three things to remember
- **Don't build too much** — focus on 3–4 core features.
- **Stage your prompts** — scaffold first, then auth, then features.
- **Use real users and real data** — it proves the thing actually works.
